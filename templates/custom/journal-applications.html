Move your journal to the OLH. We welcome expressions of interest from subscription-based journals interested in moving to diamond open access.

As a diamond open access publisher, we will never demand any payments from your authors. When you join the OLH, we commit to publishing your journal on our platform in perpetuity.

We’ve spent 10 years building a sustainable business model for funding diamond open access publishing. Because we have our own in-house publishing platform, we can offer our journals cutting-edge technology at a sustainable cost for our international library partners, ensuring the OLH’s sustainable growth into the future.

Journals interested in applying to join the OLH should be:

  • Peer-reviewed.
  • Established, with an archive of at least five years.
  • Currently funded through a subscription model.
  • Based in a humanities or humanities-related social sciences discipline.
  • Internationally recognised, with a geographically diverse editorial board.

If you feel your journal fits the above criteria and you are interested in applying for the journal to join the Open Library of Humanities, please download and complete our expression of interest form.

Our capacity to take on new journals depends on generous, worldwide support from libraries and institutions. As we commit to publishing our journals in perpetuity, we are careful that we grow sustainably. Applications are reviewed periodically by our OLH Editorial Board in a competitive process.

If your application passes the first stage of the application process, we will send the application to external review. This will require full access to the journal archives for peer reviewers to complete their review.
